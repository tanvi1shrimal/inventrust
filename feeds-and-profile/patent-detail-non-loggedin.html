Bite Block System

This method teaches how to recover and reuse a powder of a thermally degradable material which is used in a selective laser sintering (SLS) process. To recycle such a laser-sinterable powder successfully in an SLS machine it is protected against degradation by cooling the hot bed while layer-upon-layer (layer- wise) of powder is being sintered, seriatim, to form successive slices fused together to form an article of arbitrary shape (part). The process requires maintaining the powder which has more than 80 % of the number of particles 53 mum as a quiescent bed which is nevertheless porous enough to permit flowing a cooling gas downwardly through the bed without disrupting its surface.
