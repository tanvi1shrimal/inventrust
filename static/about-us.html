About Us

Invent the Near Future with Us

Inventrust makes it easy for organizations and individuals to connect with and support inventors who can help them build large value. Generating innovation with strong commercial value and social impact within most industries is a cumbersome effort fraught with enormous complexity. We are on a mission to accelerate it. After years spent helping both large multinationals enhance their portfolio of inventions and small start-ups turbocharge their intellectual property, we have come to grasp one essential truth: it takes a “village" to build a valuable innovation asset. We are building a market network focused on growing such innovation ecosystems.

Inventrust allows members of its market network to discover relevant inventions, connect with the people behind these inventions, enlist their help and expertise in inventing what they want, commercialize and raise money for these inventions, and build the near future.

Our platform, social protocols and analytical tools are designed to democratize innovation by leveling the playing field among innovators – whether they are working in garage labs or in corporate campuses. Our platform and services treat innovators fairly, help them overcome barriers of geography or funding, and are integral to helping them enhance their careers. Whenever connected communities learning from each other, standardized rules of engagement, and professional tools converge, innovation takes giant leaps. While we expect that this approach will help us drive a highly profitable innovation platform, it will also help us build our most valuable and enduring asset: trust. The trust of its globally connected members will make Inventrust grow into the world’s first scalable innovation collective.

InvenTrust Team and Advisors

We bring a track record of scaling high growth ventures, managing intellectual property, leading innovation, and delivering social impact. We treat innovation as a passionate pursuit, not a corporate platitude, and tie our business to the success of the innovators who we serve. Our team and advisors work from industrial labs, home offices, co-working spaces, cafes, and academic centers throughout the world united by a passion for innovation and a desire to help those who are involved with invention.
